How to play: use the arrow keys (swipe for mobile) to move the pieces. When pieces with the same number move into each other, the pieces merge and double.

Multiplayer: The two players take turns. On your turn, merge one of your pieces with one of your opponents to steal the piece. Merging two of your own pieces or two of your opponent's doesn't change the ownership. The winner is the first to reach 64 or fill up the board with the highest score.
